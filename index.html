FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need a credit card to start? +
No. The Free plan is permanently free — no credit card required. You can start creating content and scheduling posts right away.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your data is always exportable.
Which platforms are supported? +
X (Twitter), Facebook Pages, Instagram Business, LinkedIn, and Threads. OAuth connections are one-click and no passwords are ever stored.
How does WhatsApp work? +
Connect your WhatsApp Business number to PostForge. Then send voice notes or text messages describing what content you need. The AI transcribes your voice note, generates platform-specific posts, and sends them back for your approval. Approve with a reply — we handle the rest.
Is Naira pricing permanent? +
Yes. We're building for the Nigerian market. Prices are in ₦ and won't flip to USD. What you sign up for is what you pay, period.
