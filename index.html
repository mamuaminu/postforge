How it works

From idea to published — in minutes

A streamlined flow that turns a single content brief into fully optimised posts across every platform.

1

Describe your topic

Type a content brief — a product launch, an insight, a promotion. GPT-4o does the rest.

2

AI generates variants

Platform-native versions for X, Facebook, Instagram, Threads, and LinkedIn. Each optimised for its audience.

3

Review & approve

Preview all variants. Edit inline. Approve individual posts or bulk-approve for scheduling.

4

Schedule or publish

Post now or queue for optimal times. AI suggests best windows based on your audience's history.

5

Track performance

Real-time analytics per platform. Engagement rates, impressions, clicks — all in one dashboard.
